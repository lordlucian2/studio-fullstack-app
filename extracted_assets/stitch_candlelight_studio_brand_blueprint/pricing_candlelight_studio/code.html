Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my plan later? add
Absolutely. Most of our clients start on the Starter tier and migrate to Growth as their organic traffic scales. We handle the transition seamlessly with no downtime to your strategy or operations.
How long until I see SEO results? add
Technical SEO is a long-term investment. While performance gains (speed, indexability) are immediate, organic keyword ranking improvements typically materialize within 3-6 months. The Growth plan accelerates this through more aggressive content and backlink strategies.
Is there a minimum contract length? add
To maintain the quality and strategy depth Candlelight Studio is known for, we operate on a 6-month minimum commitment for SEO-intensive plans. Design-only projects are billed per milestone.
What is included in Bespoke Enterprise? add
The Enterprise tier is a custom-scoped partnership. It typically involves international SEO strategies, complex multi-site architecture management, and full-time dedicated design and engineering resources.
